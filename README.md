# Titan Pilot Research

Public research, evidence standards, benchmarks, and reproducible studies for Supervised AI Trading.

## Mission

Titan Pilot Research exists to publish claims that can be inspected, challenged, and reproduced.

It does not publish trading hype, anonymous win-rate claims, selective backtests, or model-generated conclusions without evidence.

## Scope

This repository may contain:

- decision-governance research
- AI validation studies
- prompt/model evaluation
- provider reliability benchmarks
- replay and determinism evidence
- operational resilience reports
- market-structure studies
- competitor and category research
- public specifications
- sanitized datasets and fixtures
- methodology and negative results

## Repository Contract

- `Titan` owns engineering truth.
- `LaunchOS` owns company, messaging, sales, and operating playbooks.
- `Portfolio` owns public presentation at titanpilot.app.
- `Research` owns public evidence and reproducible publications.

Research must not duplicate private engineering documentation or commercial strategy. It cites their public-safe outputs where necessary.

## Publication Classes

### R1 — Reproducible Result

Includes method, data or fixture, code/config version, limitations, and reproduction steps.

### R2 — Evidence Report

Documents a real operational or product event with primary evidence, but may rely on a private system or redacted data.

### R3 — Research Note

A reasoned analysis or market/category study. Clearly distinguishes sourced facts, assumptions, and opinion.

### R4 — Draft

Unreviewed work. Must be visibly labeled and must not be cited as an established Titan Pilot claim.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- Negative results are retained.
- Sample size is always shown.
- Backtests are never presented as forward evidence.
- Hypothetical outcomes are labeled.
- Model output is not treated as a source.
- Every metric defines its numerator and denominator.
- Every publication names its limitations.
- Material conflicts of interest are disclosed.
- Public claims must link to a source or be removed.

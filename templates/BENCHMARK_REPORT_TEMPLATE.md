# BM-XXXX — Benchmark Title

## Scope
Capability tested, systems/versions, date, environments, and exclusions.

## Frozen protocol
Task, fixtures, repetitions, allowed retries, timeout, terminal outcomes, scoring rubric, and stop conditions.

## Fairness statement
Explain why the comparison is materially equivalent and identify unavoidable asymmetries.

## Metrics
Counts, pass rate, false accept/reject, latency distribution, cost, operator effort, and missing runs.

## Results table
Show every system and every measured metric. Mark unavailable, not tested, and failed distinctly.

## Failure analysis
Classify infrastructure, contract, quality, safety, configuration, and measurement failures.

## Reproduction
Scripts, fixture hashes, versions, commands, raw-result manifest, and access restrictions.

## Limitations
Terms/access constraints, sample size, temporal drift, vendor-version uncertainty, and conflicts.

## Claims
Only claims directly supported by the protocol, each with evidence level and review date.

## Vendor correction record
Record contacted date, response, accepted corrections, and unresolved disagreements.
